Patrons must use their valid ID card to access library services and resources.

Return

  • Members must return/renew items borrowed within the due date range.
  • Items must be returned to BAIUST Central Library,Bangladesh Army International University of Science & Technology

Overdue

  • Items will be overdue if not returned or renewed by the due date.
  • Borrowing will be stopped until overdue items are returned and fines are paid.

Holds

  • Holds can be placed for checked-out materials through the online catalog
  • borrowers will be notified when the item is available for pick-up.

Fines

Tk. 5.00 (Five) only per calendar day for each Book item (BK).

Lost Items

If an item is not returned within 6 weeks, it is considered lost.

  • Replace it with a new copy or the latest edition.
  • Or pay double the current price of the item.
